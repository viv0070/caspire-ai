Founder Story

We are CA students ourselves.

We know what it's like to stare at a huge syllabus, miss study targets, feel overwhelmed, and wonder whether we're studying the right topics.

CAspire AI started with a simple belief:

Every CA student deserves a personal mentor available 24/7.

Not everyone can afford expensive coaching, personal guidance, or mentorship.

We are building CAspire AI to help students plan smarter, stay consistent, track progress, and achieve their goals with confidence.

We're starting with CA Foundation and aim to support students throughout their entire CA journey.
